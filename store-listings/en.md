# Flowpedia — store listing (English)

## Tagline
Flowpedia — a social way to explore Wikipedia.

## Short description
Flowpedia turns Wikipedia into a feed you actually want to scroll. Like, save, and share articles you love, then tap any highlighted word to bounce straight to the next topic. One curiosity leads to another.

## Google Play Store
🌍 Flowpedia turns Wikipedia into a feed you actually want to scroll.

Open the app and dive into an endless stream of fascinating articles. Like the ones you love, save them for later, and tap any highlighted word to jump straight to the next topic — one curiosity always leads to another.

✨ What you can do:
📜 Scroll an infinite, personalized feed of Wikipedia articles
👍 Like and 🔖 save articles to read later, even offline
🔗 Tap inline links to bounce from topic to topic
🧭 Switch between For You, Popular, News and the immersive Flow view
🔎 Search and explore millions of articles
🎯 Tune your interests so the feed fits you
🌙 Light, dark and high-contrast themes
🌐 Available in 14 languages
♿ Designed with accessibility in mind

Every article links back to its Wikipedia source. Content comes from Wikipedia, available under CC BY-SA.

Start exploring — you never know where you'll end up.
